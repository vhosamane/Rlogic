About RLogic

RLogic Technologies Established in the year 2019, we are situated in Bangalore. Based on its rich experience and technology understanding, with own production capability and centralized resource locally, we are able to offer you one stop service with small, medium to mass production with competitive terms, quality and delivery assurance that customized all your request
RLogic Technologies group include: Aerospace and defense industries; Electronics Industries; Automation and Robotic industries.
Rlogic Technologies provides endless solutions to your design and manufacturing needs. We experienced to produce excellence we are your direct source for PCB Fabrication and Wireharness Fabrication requirements.
