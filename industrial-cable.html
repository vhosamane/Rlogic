Industrial cables

With its industry-leading and diverse product offering, Sansri is positioned to meet the challenges faced by today's industrial automation customers. Our customers include automotive and consumer products manufacturers, plants and facilities management, industrial equipment manufacturers, health care and medical device manufacturers, and maintenance and repair operations. Our industrial automation products comply with major safety and performance standards and regulations.


Through innovative design and engineering expertise, Sansri provides you with reliable wire and cable products, which include Instrumentation & Control Cables; Industrial Communications Cables for Protocols; Industrial Ethernet Cables; Portable Power & Cord Products; and VFD Cables for Drives.


All are designed to deliver ongoing productivity in the harshest of industrial environments.


Sansri's family of high performing industrial cables support a wide range of applications and are designed to meet or exceed performance requirements for rugged industrial environments. Sansri offers custom and standard cables and retractable cords for industrial instrumentation, factory automation, data, and network systems featuring signal, control, instrumentation and power. Our extensive range of industrial wires & cables includes products such as control cables, data and computer cables, drag chain cables, motor, servo & feedback cables, all-weather and rubber cables, and single cores, as well as internationally approved cables.
